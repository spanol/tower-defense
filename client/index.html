Features

5 Tower Types

Arrow, Cannon, Frost, Lightning, and Mortar — each with 3 upgrade tiers and unique mechanics.

3 Maps

Forest Path, Desert Crossing, and Mountain Pass. Each with unique layouts and strategic chokepoints.

Real-time Multiplayer

Join rooms with 4-character codes. Server-authoritative at 20 ticks/sec with delta compression.

Boss Waves

Every 5th wave brings a boss. Five enemy types with armor, flight, and speed variants.

Economy System

Gold interest, wave bonuses, and tower selling. Manage resources to survive the late game.

Instant Play

Runs entirely in the browser. No downloads, no accounts, no installs. Just click and play.

Game Modes

⚔

Solo

Classic tower defense. Pick a map, place towers, survive all waves.

⚑

Co-op (2–4 players)

Shared economy and lives. Coordinate tower placement and upgrades with your team.

⚔️

Versus (1v1)

Per-player lanes with attack tokens. Outlast your opponent by sending enemies their way.
